Ready to Discover Florence?


Do you want to discover the "real" places to visit in the city?

From Leonardo Da Vinci to Calcio Storico, Florence has everything you need.
Walk the same streets as many artists, scientist and authors.
Da Vinci, Dante & Galileo all have lived and walked down these streets before us.

Modern Florence is also a sight to see!
Enjoy the clash between historical and modern city.
